Our Purpose

Our Mission

ICANJ is a welcoming community in Hackensack, NJ — a place to find hope, build real relationships, and grow at your own pace. We've been rooted in northern New Jersey for over 25 years.

Multigenerational and multicultural, we honour our Indian heritage while remaining open to every person who walks through our doors — wherever they're starting from.

Rooted in the Word
Every sermon, decision, and programme is measured against Scripture alone.
Gathered to Glorify
We come to encounter God — not to observe a religious performance.
Better Together
Authentic relationships built on love, accountability, and mutual encouragement.
Welcome to All
Every person who walks through our doors is made in the image of God.
Doctrine

What We Believe

We're a Bible-believing church. Here's the short version of our core convictions — the foundation everything else is built on.

01
The Bible
The inspired, inerrant Word of God and the supreme authority for faith and life.
02
The Trinity
One God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
03
Jesus Christ
Fully God and fully man — born of a virgin, sinless, died in our place, rose bodily.
04
Salvation
By grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone. Freely given by God.
05
The Holy Spirit
Indwells every believer, produces Scripture's fruit, empowers the Church for mission.
06
The Church
The body of Christ — called to worship, discipleship, fellowship, and mission.

Our History

Our Story

1998
Foundations Laid
A small group of Indian Christian families in Hackensack began gathering in homes for worship and prayer, planting the seed of what would become ICANJ.
2003
First Permanent Home
ICANJ was formally established and moved into its first dedicated meeting space in northern New Jersey, marking a new chapter of growth.
2010
Growth & Expansion
Membership grew substantially. Dedicated children's and youth ministries were launched to serve every generation with intentional discipleship.
2024
Sermons Go Online
Weekly Bible study teaching became available on Spotify and Apple Podcasts, extending our reach well beyond the walls of our Hackensack campus.
