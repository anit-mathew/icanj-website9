What to Expect

Your First Sunday

Here's everything you need to feel at home before you even walk through the door.

🅿️
Parking
Free parking is available in the lot adjacent to the building. Street parking is also available on State St and nearby side streets. Our parking volunteers are on-site before each service to help you find a spot.
👗
What to Wear
Come as you are! No dress code here. Some people dress up, others keep it casual — what matters is that you're here. You'll see everything from jeans to Sunday best.
🙌
Service Style
Our Sunday gatherings blend heartfelt worship with Spirit-filled preaching. Expect a warm, participatory atmosphere — the presence of God is central to everything we do.
⏱
Service Length
About 1.5 hours.
🎵
Worship Style
A mix of contemporary praise and traditional hymns — music that moves both the heart and the spirit.
📖
Preaching Style
Practical, Bible-based messages rooted in the Word of God. Relatable, encouraging, and designed to speak to real life. Bring your Bible — or follow along on your phone.
👦
Kids Ministry

Your Kids Are in Good Hands

We have a full Kids Ministry running during Sunday service so your children can learn about Jesus in a fun, age-appropriate, and safe environment. Kids are checked in before service and will have their own worship, teaching, and activities tailored just for them.
